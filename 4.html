Bound insert delivery

Packing Requirements

  • Inserts must be stacked the same way up and same way round if packed flat in cartons
  • If bundling, please plastic strip wrap across the bundle only
  • DO NOT double tie from head to foot
  • Sections with laps, or pasted areas must be packed so that sections or laps do not curl
  • A stack, or a turn within a stack, must NOT be less than 20 copies
  • Cartons are to be no heavier than 20kg
  • Inserts should be stacked on standard TNT or Chep pallets
  • All pallets must not exceed maximum weight of 1,100kg
  • Maximum height not exceeding 1,200mm (1.2 metres)
  • For smaller deliveries skids are acceptable
  • MUST be stacked separately and clearly labelled

Delivery requirements

  • All deliveries must be correctly labelled with the Australian Doctor insert delivery label
  • Any product that is delivered without the minimum information may be rejected

Delivery deadline

  • All inserts are due on the Friday two weeks prior to the publication date
