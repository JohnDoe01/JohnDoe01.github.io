Media requirements

  • Client must purchase an advertisement when booking a tip-on

Size size

  • Subject to trial run/testing and mailhouse approval

Stock

  • Dependent on product
  • Minimum 80gsm

Tip on page positioning

This publication is crash-folded therefore the tip-on must be positioned a minimum of 15mm from either side of the centre fold

Samples

  • 10 samples of the tip-on must be supplied for a trial run
  • Costs may increase if the delivered product does not match the sample provided (as a higher cost premium to cover extra labour and time delays may be applied)

Packing requirements

  • Tip-ons must be stacked the same way up and same way round then packed flat in cartons
  • If bundling, please plastic strip wrap across the bundle only
  • DO NOT double tie from head to foot
  • Tip-ons must be packed so that they do not curl
  • Inserts must be stacked on standard TNT or Chep pallets to a height not exceeding 1.2m
  • Inserts for multiple issues MUST be stacked on separate pallets and clearly labelled
  • Weigh no more than 1,100kg and not exceeding height of 1,200mm (1.2 metres)
