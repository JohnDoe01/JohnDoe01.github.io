Position

  • Onserts appear outside the publication, under the carrier sheet

Requirements

  • Onserts must not be in the form of a letter, or contain any saluations e.g. Dear Doctor, Dear Sir, Yours sincerely
  • Inserts may be subject to approval by Australia Post

Maximum inserts

  • Three loose onserts per issue (all onserts are subject to approval)

Minumum supply size

  • 148mm (h) x 105mm (w) Anything smaller will be subject to trial and approval

Maximum supply size and stock weight

  • Same size as publication once it has been crashfolded
  • Optimum size is 270mm (h) x 190mm (w)
  • Larger onserts must be supplied folded down to this size or smaller

Pagination Minimum Maximum
Single Leaf 80gsm 130gsm
Four pages 80gsm 130gsm
Eight pages plus 80gsm 100gsm

Folding

  • Folded inserts must have a ‘closed’ edge to enable automatic feeding on the collator
  • Concertina ‘Z’ folds may be onserted by hand at an increased cost

Samples

  • The previous information is intended as a guide only. It may be possible to onsert products that fall outside these guidelines
  • Contact Production who will be able to advise on the suitability of any samples you submit
  • SAMPLES OF ALL PROPOSED ONSERTS MUST BE SUBMITTED TO PRODUCTION FOR APPROVAL BEFORE PROCEEDING WITH ORDER
  • Creative cannot masquerade as editorial and must be submitted to us at least four weeks before onsertion to obtain approval before proceeding

Packing requirements

  • Onserts must be stacked same way up and same way round and packed flat in cartons
  • If bundling, please plastic strip wrap across the bundle only
  • DO NOT double tie from head to foot
  • Sections with laps, or pasted areas must be packed so that sections or laps do not curl
  • A stack, or a turn within a stack, must NOT be less than 20 copies
  • Cartons must not exceed 14kg each
  • Magazines and large brochures should be stacked on standard TNT or Chep Pallets
  • Weigh no more than 1,100kg and not exceeding height of 1.2m

Delivery requirements

  • Delivery labels must be used for all deliveries
  • Delivery times: Monday - Friday 7am - 4pm
