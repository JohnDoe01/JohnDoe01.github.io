Maximum inserts available per issue

  • One centre-bound insert
  • Two section bound inserts
  • One side collate (Must be 4pp A3 - full page)

Minimum untrimmed supply size

  • 190mm (h) x 145mm (w) (plus flap*)
  • Minimum size includes a 5mm foot trim allowance

Maximum untrimmed supply size

  • 410mm (h) x 305mm (w)
  • Maximum size includes a 5mm foot trim allowance and head trim allowance and 25mm foredge trim allowance

Binding flap

  • As per individual bound insert specifications

Closed edge

  • Multiple page inserts also need to have a closed foot and be delivered folded

Recommended stock

Single leaf with flap 4 pages 8 pages
100gsm - 130gsm 100gsm - 130gsm 60gsm - 80gsm

Bleed / type area

  • Any part of an insert, where the image is required to bleed off the page should have a minimum 5mm bleed allowance
  • When designing inserts to maximum acceptable sizes, be sure that type and any 'live' material is kept 5mm from the trim
  • We recommend that no 'live' material be closer than 5mm from trim edge all round

Small bound cards (eg, subs card)

  • 190mm (h) x 145mm (w) (must be folded)
  • Minimum includes 5mm foot trim (knocks to the foot)

Folding

  • Folded inserts must have a 'closed' edge to enable automatic feeding on the collator
  • Concertina (Z) folds may be suitable with additional gluing (subject to approval)
